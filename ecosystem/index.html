Ecosystem

  • There are 53 core plugins and 191 community plugins
  • A core plugin is a plugin maintained by the fastify team, and we make our best to maintain them according to https://github.com/fastify/fastify/blob/master/Reference/docs/LTS.md.
  • We guarantee that every community plugin respects Fastify best practices (tests, etc) at the time they have been added to the list. We offer no guarantee on their maintenance.
  • Can't you find the plugin you are looking for? No problem, it's very easy to write one!
